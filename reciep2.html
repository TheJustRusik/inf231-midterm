Fragrant Risotto with Truffle Oil

Difficulty:

Easy

Ð¡ooking time:

20min

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cucumber, cut into rings
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • Salt, pepper taste and Juice of half lemon
  • 2 tbsp. spoons olive oil

Preparation:

  • Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook in salted water until tender.
  • Combine quinoa with avocado, cucumber, onion and cilantro.
  • Season with salt, pepper, lemon juice and olive oil.
