Thai Tom Yum Goong Soup

Difficulty:

Hard

Ð¡ooking time:

40min

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter of chicken broth.
  • 200 g shrimp, peeled
  • 200 g shiitake mushrooms, chopped
  • 2 lemon leaves
  • 2 lemongrass stalks, crushed
  • 3 tbsp. spoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. spoons nam plaa (Thai fish sauce)
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons red curry
  • 1 teaspoon tomato puree
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro for serving

Preparation:

  • Boil the broth, add lemongrass and lemon leaves.
  • Add shrimp, mushrooms, soy sauce, nam plaa, sugar, red curry and tomato puree.
  • Cook over medium heat for 15-20 minutes.
  • Add lime juice and serve garnished with fresh cilantro.
