Fragrant Risotto with Truffle Oil

Difficulty:

Medium

Ð¡ooking time:

30min

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1 liter chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 glass white wine
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp. spoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt, pepper to taste
  • Truffle oil for serving

Preparation:

  • In a saucepan, fry the onion in olive oil until soft.
  • Add rice and fry until translucent.
  • Pour in the wine and cook until completely absorbed.
  • Gradually add broth, stirring until rice is tender.
  • Add Parmesan, salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve the risotto with a drizzle of truffle oil.
